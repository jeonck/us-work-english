# 커버레터 작성법

## 구조 (3단락)

### 1단락 — 지원 이유

```
I am excited to apply for the Software Engineer position at Acme Corp.
With 5 years of experience in full-stack development and a strong passion
for building user-centric products, I believe I would be a great fit for your team.
```

### 2단락 — 핵심 경험 1~2개

```
In my previous role at XYZ Company, I led the migration of our monolithic
application to microservices, which reduced deployment time by 60% and
improved system reliability to 99.9% uptime. I also mentored two junior
engineers who are now leading their own projects.
```

### 3단락 — 마무리

```
I am eager to bring my skills and experience to Acme Corp and contribute
to your mission of making software more accessible. I would love the
opportunity to discuss how I can add value to your team.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
```

---

## 유용한 표현

| 상황 | 표현 |
|------|------|
| 지원 이유 | I am excited / thrilled / eager to apply for... |
| 경험 소개 | In my X years of experience... / During my time at... |
| 강점 강조 | I have a proven track record of... |
| 회사 언급 | I admire [Company]'s commitment to... |
| 마무리 | I look forward to discussing this opportunity... |

:::caution 피해야 할 표현
- ❌ "I am writing to apply..." (너무 딱딱하고 흔함)
- ❌ 이력서 내용을 그대로 반복
- ❌ "I think I might be a good fit..." (자신감 부족)
:::
