# 미국 취업 인터뷰

## STAR 답변 구조

행동 면접(Behavioral Interview)의 황금 공식입니다.

| 요소 | 의미 | 예시 |
|------|------|------|
| **S**ituation | 상황 설명 | "At my previous company, we had a critical bug in production..." |
| **T**ask | 내 역할 | "I was responsible for diagnosing and fixing the issue..." |
| **A**ction | 내가 한 행동 | "I traced the root cause to a race condition and implemented a mutex lock..." |
| **R**esult | 결과 (숫자) | "This reduced error rates by 95% and restored service within 2 hours." |

---

## 자주 나오는 질문과 답변 포인트

### "Tell me about yourself."
```
I'm a [role] with [X years] of experience in [area]. Most recently, I worked at
[Company] where I [key achievement]. Before that, I [brief background].
I'm now looking for an opportunity to [what you want next].
```

### "Why do you want to work here?"
```
I've been following [Company] for a while because [specific reason — product,
mission, or culture]. I was particularly impressed by [specific thing].
I believe my experience in [skill] aligns well with what your team is building.
```

### "Tell me about a challenge you faced."
> STAR 구조로 답변. 실패처럼 보이는 경험도 괜찮습니다 — **배운 점**이 핵심입니다.

### "Where do you see yourself in 5 years?"
```
I want to grow as a [engineer/leader/specialist] and take on more responsibility
over time. I'm excited about the opportunity here because [Company] seems like
a place where I can [grow/lead/build].
```

---

## 인터뷰에서 질문하기

면접 마지막에 반드시 질문하세요 — 관심 있다는 신호입니다.

```
What does success look like in this role in the first 90 days?
What are the biggest challenges the team is facing right now?
How would you describe the engineering culture here?
What do you enjoy most about working at [Company]?
```

## 감사 이메일 (Thank-you Email)

인터뷰 후 24시간 이내에 보내세요.

```
Subject: Thank You – [Job Title] Interview

Hi [Name],

Thank you so much for taking the time to speak with me today about
the [Job Title] role. I really enjoyed learning about [specific topic
discussed] and the team's approach to [something they mentioned].

Our conversation reinforced my excitement about this opportunity, and
I'm confident that my experience in [skill] would allow me to contribute
meaningfully from day one.

Please let me know if you need any additional information. I look forward
to hearing from you!

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```
