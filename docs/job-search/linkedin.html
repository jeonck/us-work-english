프로필 최적화​

Headline (헤드라인)​

직함 하나보다 가치를 보여주세요.

❌ Software Engineer at XYZ
✅ Full-Stack Engineer | React & Node.js | Building scalable products

About (자기소개)​

I'm a software engineer with 5+ years of experience helping startups
and mid-size companies build reliable, user-friendly web applications.

I specialize in React, Node.js, and cloud infrastructure (AWS). I thrive
in collaborative environments and enjoy mentoring junior developers.

Currently open to Senior Engineer or Tech Lead roles in SF Bay Area or remote.

네트워킹 메시지 템플릿​

처음 연결 요청​

Hi [Name],

I came across your profile while researching [Company/field]. I'm a
[Your role] with experience in [skill], and I'd love to connect and
learn from your journey.

Looking forward to connecting!
[Your name]

커피챗 요청​

Hi [Name],

Thank you for connecting! I've been following your work at [Company]
and I'm really impressed by [specific project/post].

Would you be open to a 20-minute virtual coffee chat? I'd love to
learn more about your experience in [field/role].

No worries if you're too busy — I really appreciate your time either way.

Best,
[Your name]

채용담당자(Recruiter)에게 연락할 때​

Hi [Recruiter Name],

I noticed you're hiring for the [Job Title] role at [Company]. I have
[X years] of experience in [relevant skills] and I'm very interested
in this opportunity.

I'd love to learn more about the role and the team. Would you have
15 minutes for a quick call this week?

Thank you!
[Your name]
