미팅 영어

회의 시작할 때​

Alright, let's get started.
Thanks everyone for joining. Let's jump in.
Before we begin, does everyone have the agenda?

의견 말할 때​

I think we should consider...
From my perspective, ...
One thing I'd like to add is...
Building on what [Name] said, ...

동의/반대할 때​

동의​

That makes a lot of sense.
I agree with that approach.
Absolutely. That's a great point.

부드럽게 반대​

I see your point, but I wonder if we should also consider...
That's a good idea. My only concern is...
I'm not sure I fully agree. Could we look at this from another angle?

잘 못 들었을 때​

Sorry, could you repeat that?
I didn't quite catch that — could you say it again?
Could you clarify what you mean by [term]?

발언 기회 잡기​

If I can jump in here...
Can I add something quickly?
Sorry to interrupt, but I think this is important...

회의 마무리​

Let's wrap up here.
To summarize what we decided today...
The action items are: [list]. Does that sound right to everyone?
Let's reconnect on this [next week / Thursday].

자주 쓰는 회의 표현​

표현의미
"Take this offline"따로 얘기하자
"Circle back"나중에 다시 논의하자
"On the same page"같은 이해를 공유함
"Move the needle"진전을 만들다
"Table this for now"일단 보류하자
"Touch base"간단히 확인하다

:::caution 회의 중 흔한 실수 침묵을 두려워해서 아무 말도 못하는 것보다, 짧게라도 "That makes sense" 또는 "I agree"라고 말하는 게 좋습니다. :::
