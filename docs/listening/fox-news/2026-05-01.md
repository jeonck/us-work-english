# 2026년 5월 1일 — Trade Tariffs

## 오늘의 헤드라인

> **"White House Defends Sweeping Tariffs as Markets React"**

---

## 핵심 단어 & 표현

| 단어 / 표현 | 뜻 | 예문 |
|---|---|---|
| **sweeping** | 광범위한, 전면적인 | *sweeping tariffs on all imports* |
| **tariff** | 관세 | *a 25% tariff on steel* |
| **impose** | 부과하다 | *The U.S. imposed new tariffs.* |
| **retaliate** | 보복하다 | *China threatened to retaliate.* |
| **trade deficit** | 무역 적자 | *The trade deficit widened last quarter.* |
| **market volatility** | 시장 변동성 | *Market volatility spiked after the announcement.* |
| **supply chain** | 공급망 | *Supply chains were disrupted overnight.* |

---

## 방송 발췌 (섀도잉용)

아래 문장들을 앵커 목소리처럼 또렷하고 자신 있게 읽어본다.

> *"The White House is standing firm on its decision to impose sweeping tariffs, saying the move is necessary to protect American jobs and reduce the trade deficit."*

> *"Markets reacted sharply, with the Dow falling more than 400 points in early trading as investors weighed the risk of a prolonged trade war."*

> *"Beijing has vowed to retaliate, announcing plans to place tariffs on U.S. agricultural exports, a move that could hit farmers in key swing states."*

## 관련 표현 더 보기

- *level the playing field* — 공정한 경쟁 환경을 만들다
- *tit-for-tat* — 맞대응, 보복 (관세전쟁에서 자주 등장)
- *economic headwinds* — 경제적 역풍 (성장을 막는 부정적 요인)
