자주 쓰는 비즈니스 이디엄

미국 직장에서 자주 들리는 표현들입니다. 알아듣기만 해도 큰 도움이 됩니다.

회의·업무 표현​

표현의미예문
Hit the ground running바로 성과를 내다"We need someone who can hit the ground running."
Low-hanging fruit쉽게 할 수 있는 것"Let's start with the low-hanging fruit."
Bandwidth여유/시간"Do you have the bandwidth to take this on?"
On my radar인지하고 있음"That's on my radar."
Deep dive깊이 분석하다"Let's do a deep dive into the data."
Synergy시너지 (협력 효과)"There's real synergy between the two teams."
Boil the ocean불필요하게 크게 하다"Don't boil the ocean — just fix the core issue."

진행·일정 표현​

표현의미예문
Touch base간단히 확인하다"Let's touch base on Friday."
Circle back나중에 다시 논의하다"Can we circle back on this tomorrow?"
Move the needle진전을 만들다"We need something that actually moves the needle."
In the pipeline진행 중인"We have several features in the pipeline."
Drop the ball실수하다 / 놓치다"I dropped the ball on that deadline, sorry."
On the same page같은 이해를 공유함"Are we all on the same page?"
