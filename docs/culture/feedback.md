# 피드백 주고받기

## 피드백 받을 때

미국 직장에서 피드백은 공격이 아닌 **성장의 기회**입니다.

```
감사하게 받기:
"Thank you for the feedback. That's really helpful."
"I appreciate you letting me know. I'll work on that."

더 명확히 알고 싶을 때:
"Could you give me a specific example?"
"What would that look like in practice?"
"How would you approach this differently?"
```

---

## 피드백 줄 때

### SBI 모델 (Situation - Behavior - Impact)

```
Situation:  "In yesterday's meeting..."
Behavior:   "When you interrupted [Name] while they were speaking..."
Impact:     "it made it harder for the team to hear their idea, and I think
             it may have discouraged others from speaking up."
```

### 긍정적 피드백 주기

```
"I wanted to call out the great job you did on the [project].
The way you handled [specific thing] really made a difference."

"I noticed how you [behavior] — that's exactly the kind of thing
that makes our team stronger. Keep it up!"
```

### 건설적 피드백 주기

```
"I have some feedback on [topic]. Is now a good time?"
"I think there's an opportunity to improve [area]. Would you be open
to hearing my thoughts?"
```

## 성과 평가(Performance Review) 준비

```
자기 평가(Self-review) 잘 쓰는 법:
- 구체적인 프로젝트 + 숫자로 증명
- "I contributed to..." 보다 "I led... which resulted in..."
- 도전했던 것과 배운 점 솔직하게

목표 설정(Goal Setting):
"My goal for next quarter is to [specific, measurable goal]."
예: "I want to reduce our deployment time by 30% by implementing CI/CD improvements."
```
